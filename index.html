Arcade Game

Instructions

  • Use arrow keys to move Player
  • Avoid Rocks and Bugs
  • Collect Gems for extra points
  • A Win is earned when Player reaches water
  • Enemies and Gems Respawn after Win

Score

  • Gems + 50pts
  • Hits - 25pts
  • Reach Water + 100pts

Stats

  • Gems: 0
  • Hits: 0
  • Wins: 0
  • Score: 0
